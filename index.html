About The Show

The East Dove is a Christian television program which is aimed to outreach to all. The show will have the people actively seek out answers to questions about different aspects of life.

From spiritual growth to marital issues, ehtnic divides, socio-economic progress and shared prosperity to security and the rule of law - all these will be part of the issuestackled in the show, to provide a divine solution to all solutions.

I saw many doves coming from the eastern side and swiftly covered the whole world.
Their wings were made out of iron and everyone knelt at the sight of them lest they might be cut off.

The dove in this case symbolises the Holy Spirit, Mathew 3:16 (Religion, Traditions, Customs and Doctrine of men MUST give way as per the word of God).

Target

The Show will be a platform which will target to bring all on board regardless of socio-economic status, religion, gender, race, ethnicity to the full knowledge of the good news of the unconditional love of God and His will for all to prosper spiritually, physically and materially
The show will be on air in Kenya on local media, and will expand regionally as opportunities open up. The episodes will also be posted on YouTube page, The East Dove Show
It features interviews by people in the street, experts and everyone in between. Using a pragmatic, divinely-inspired approach, Apostle Mary Magdalene Wangui provides insight into these issues and layout inclusive solutions for each by the grace of God. God is not in the periphery of the human society but He is interwoven in its fabric (Psalms 46:5)
